# Avorion Wiki

A player-facing wiki for **Avorion**, focused on game mechanics derived directly from the game's scripts.

## Economy & Trading

- **[Trading and Prices](Trading-and-Prices)** – how station prices are calculated (supply/demand, relations, tax)
- **[Goods](Goods)** – the full commodity catalog, raw resources, and contraband
- **[Production](Production)** – every factory recipe and the build/upgrade cost formulas
- **[Refining](Refining)** – ores and scrap into materials, and the refine fee
- **[Consumer goods](Consumer-goods)** – what each station type consumes, plus the population-profit loop
- **[Player stations](Player-stations)** – founding stations, trade settings, tax, and stock
- **[Trade Contracts](Trade-Contracts)** – automated buy-low/sell-high routes flown by a Merchant captain

## Combat & Weapons

- **[Combat](Combat)** – damage types, shields vs hull, and how weapon power scales with rarity and tech
- **[Weapons](Weapons)** – every turret weapon type, with damage type, fire rate and range
- **[Turret crafting](Turret-crafting)** – building turrets at a Turret Factory and the stat each ingredient raises
- **[Torpedoes](Torpedoes)** – torpedo bodies, warheads and damage profiles
- **[Defensive systems](Defensive-systems)** – shields, resistances, weaknesses and point defense
