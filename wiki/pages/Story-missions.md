# Story missions

Avorion has a loose **main story** that pulls you from the rim toward the **galactic core**. The
through-line is simple: the centre of the galaxy is sealed behind **the Barrier**, you need a set of
**artifacts** (teleporter keys) to open the gate through it, and those artifacts are held by various
bosses and factions scattered around the galaxy. A recurring guide — **the Adventurer**, who mails
you tips throughout — nudges you from one lead to the next.

This page is an overview of the story beats. It deliberately stays light on spoilers for the dialogue
and twists; the goal is to show what each step *is* and what it gives you.

## The shape of the story

1. **Find the artifacts.** Most of the named story missions exist to win you one **artifact**
   (a Legendary teleporter-key system upgrade). They are held by bosses and organisations across the
   galaxy.
2. **Find the gate.** With the artifacts collected, you hunt for the sector with the **eight key
   asteroids** near the Barrier, which opens the gate to the inner galaxy.
3. **Cross the Barrier** into the core region.
4. **Destroy the Guardian** — the Xsotan boss guarding the very centre — the climactic fight.

The Adventurer's mails tie these together: they congratulate you on milestones (finding Titanium,
collecting the artifacts) and point you toward the next objective.

## Story steps

These are the named scripts that make up the questline. Several are "boss" steps that each yield an
artifact; others are deliveries, investigations and contacts that set them up.

| Step | What it is |
|---|---|
| **The Hermit** | A reclusive contact who knows about the ship from the centre and the race to reach it — a key source of lore and leads. |
| **The Trading Guild** | Learn that travelling **Trade Guild** merchants will sell an artifact; get a **Trade Guild Beacon** from an Equipment Dock and use it to summon them. |
| **The Smuggler Boss** | Track down **Bottan**, the smuggling kingpin, for one of the artifacts. Involves smuggler deliveries and earning their trust. |
| **The Pirate Boss** | Hunt down **Swoks**, the pirate boss, for an artifact. |
| **The Brotherhood** | Find a bulletin about **the Brotherhood**, locate them, and defeat their leaders to take their artifact. |
| **The Scientist** | A scientist contact tied to the research into reaching the centre. |
| **The Groundbreaking Research** | Follow the research thread set up by the Scientist. |
| **The Experiment** | An **AI**-related story step (the upgraded AI built to fight what guards the centre). |
| **The Haatii** (Exodus) | The Exodus / Haatii storyline about those who fled the centre. |
| **Crossing the Barrier** | With all artifacts in hand, find the sector of **eight key asteroids** near the Barrier and open the gate to the inner galaxy. |
| **The Guardian** | Destroy the **Guardian** at the centre of the galaxy — the Xsotan boss the Resistance could never beat. The climax of the story. |

> A handful of supporting scripts back these up — artifact deliveries, collecting Xsotan technology,
> organising allies, smuggler letters, and the spawners that place the Adventurer, the Guardian and
> the galaxy's roaming bosses. You don't interact with those directly; they run the encounters the
> named steps point you at.

## Artifacts (teleporter keys)

The artifacts you collect are **Legendary system upgrades** — the **teleporter keys**. The game tracks
how many of the eight you have found (across your inventory and installed on your ships); collecting
the full set is the prerequisite for **Crossing the Barrier**. Because they are also functional system
upgrades, they have a use beyond the story.
